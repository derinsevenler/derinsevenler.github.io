Research

My research interest is in tackling at biomedical engineering questions using techniques from applied physics. I'm particularly interested in biological nanoparticles, like including viruses and extracellular vesicles, as well as molecular recognition and sensing in biological systems. I've developed new techniques to detect, measure, and manipulate individual biomolecules and bio-nanoparticles using techniques from nanophotonics and nanotechnology. My long-term vision is to use these techniques to address emerging challenges in infectious disease diagnostics, gene therapy, and systems & synthetic biology.
